Popular Destinations

In India

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Leh-Ladakh

Breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude lakes, and thrilling road trips.

Pristine white-sand beaches, scuba diving spots, and crystal-clear waters.

Kyoto

Known for its ancient temples, tranquil gardens, and stunning traditional architecture.

Santorini, Greece

Whitewashed buildings, crystal-clear waters, and mesmerizing sunsets.

New Zealand

Dramatic landscapes, adventure sports, and vibrant natural beauty.

Rome, Italy

Ancient ruins, stunning architecture, and a rich cultural heritage.
